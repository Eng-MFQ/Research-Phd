### **Reviewer Requirements**

* Must have a degree (doctoral, candidate of sciences, or a recognized foreign degree).  
* Must have publications in the relevant field of study.  
* **Internal Reviewer:** Appointed from employees of the University.  
* **External Reviewer:** Must NOT be:  
  * Employees of the University.  
  * Co-authors of published works on the topic of the dissertation.  
  * Persons who may have a conflict of interest.
