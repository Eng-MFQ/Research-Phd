**Page 25**

**Evaluation Methodology**

* LLM as a Judge: We employed an external Large Language Model, Claude, to act as a judge.

* Criterion: The judge was prompted to determine if the answers provided by the participating LLMs were semantically identical to the ground-truth answers from the domain expert.

  * \-1: They are contradictory or opposite (worng answer).

  * 0: They agree and disagree in some aspects (partially correct).

  * 1: They have identical meaning (correct).
